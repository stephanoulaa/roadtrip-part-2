Boulder

It is safe to say Colorado is one of the most beautiful places in the US, and it is ucky to have Boulder within it! With the rolling mountains, laid-back atmosphere and kind community, Boulder is also a nice proximity to other great cities like Steamboat Springs, Denver and Morrison where you can see the historic Red Rocks Amppitheatre! There is so much to do, I hope you enjoy it as much as I do!
