Chicago

Chicago, or the Windy City, is one of my favorite places in the world. With one of a kind museums, historic art work all over the city, going to a Cubs game at Wrigley, summer muscial festivals like Lollapalooza, and of course some Girodano's deep dish pizza, you can't bet it!
